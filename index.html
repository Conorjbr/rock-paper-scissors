Rock - Paper - Scissors

The Classic Game of Chance!

How To Play !

  • Make your choice by pressing the buttons
  • Computer will make a random choice
  • Computer will compare the user choice with computer choice and establish who wins

How To Win !

  • Rock smashes scissors, rock wins
  • Paper wraps around rock, paper wins
  • Scissors cuts paper, scissors wins
  • User choice and computer choice the same - result draw

See your score in the scoreboard at bottom of page

Press reset button to set scores to zero and start again
